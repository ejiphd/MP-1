Visit Intramuros Now!

The historical tour will narrate the story of the city and key happenings to the Philippines for more than 400 years. Compressed into less than 4-hour historical tour is the evolution of this place from being Malay to Muslim, to Spanish, to American, to Japanese to being a Filipino. Best if you want to know the general history of Manila and Philippines.

Visit Baguio Now!

Known as the summer capital of the Philippines, Baguio remains a must-visit tourist destination in the country through the years. The cool breeze makes it a perfect place to hang out during hot months. In fact, more tourists are coming over to the City of Pines every summer season.

Visit Vigan Now!

Vigan is a historical place. Just walking around the cobblestone streets around Calle Crisologo, or even better, taking a ride in a horse drawn carriage will take you back to the Spanish era of the Philippines.
