Best Island-Hopping Destinations in the Philippines:

TAWI-TAWI ISLAND

Tawi-Tawi is like a hidden treasure that’s yet to be discovered by travelers and is one of the best places to go island hopping in the Philippines.

PORT BARTON

Compared to the more popular destinations in Palawan like El Nido and Coron, Port Barton is yet to be discovered by more travelers.

PALAUI ISLAND

Located in the province of Sta. Ana, Cagayan, the long trip to this marvelous attraction is truly worth it as the island rewards you with a stunning landscape and seascape..
ROMBLON

Perfect to do island hopping. With the untouched natural resources that surround it, you can simply hop on a boat and witness the magic of Romblon.


DINAGAT ISLANDS

It’s that type of place that transports you to an ambiance of calmness and serenity; away from the hustle and bustle of the city life.
ONCE ISLAS

It exhibits the marvelous 11 islands along the Moro Gulf, found in barangays of Panubigan and Dita in Zamboanga del Sur.

BULALACAO

Oriental Mindoro. As more tourists discover its hidden splendor, Bulalacao is deemed to be a rising star in Mindoro travel.

CAMIGUIN ISLAND

Among the other top island destinations in the Philippines, Camiguin is famed for the unique and charming island experience it offers.

MACTAN ISLAND

Cebu.Mactan Island is not called the Diving Mecca of the South for nothing. a day exploring on a Mactan Island tour should equally make you travel with excitement.

SAMAL ISLAND

Davao del Norte. Have its own version of paradise, the island boasts of pure white sand, beautiful beaches and dramatic coastlines that leaves anyone who witness it awestruck.

PUNDAQUIT

Pundaquit island in Zambales still has that province-like ambiance to it that gives you that much-needed break from it all. You can island-hopping trip at Camara, Capones, and Potipot via Zambales tours.

MOALBOAL

Moalboal, Cebu offers an adventures that you never forget. The island’s fascinating and diverse marine life takes the center stage as it attracts more and more tourists from all over the world.


CARAMOAN ISLAND

A gem to be discovered and a paradise to be explored. There’s something special about this island.It gives you a front-row seat to witness nature’s exquisite beauty.

HUNDRED ISLANDS

True to its name, Hundred Islands in Pangasinan boasts a group of approximately 123 islets where you can go on a tropical escapade.No matter what you've yearn for, Hundred Islands got you.

PUERTO PRINCESA

Home of Underground River which is one of the 7 Wonders of Nature, a UNESCO Heritage Site, and one of the top tourist spots in Luzon, Puerto Princesa is also a must-visit tourist destination in Palawan.

BORACAY ISLAND

Boracay still is one of the best beaches for island hopping in Philippines and one of the summer destinations when it comes to fine white-sand beaches and magical sunsets.

BOHOL

Bohol surely lives up to its “Heart of the Islands” title. Embark on Bohol itinerary filled with adventures on the pretty shores of Balicasag Island and Virgin Island.
CORON

Magical lagoons, exotic lakes, fiery sunsets – these are just some of the attractions you wouldn’t want to miss when you visit the gorgeous Coron in Palawan.
SIARGAO ISLAND

Siargao has gained a special place in the heart of every traveler who visits it. When here, don't miss the chance to visit Naked Island, Daku Island, and Guyam Island.
